Summary

The CSL Hung Yen project: English Winter Fair is a student-led project that involves English teaching activities for Hung Dao primary school students within eight weeks. With the goal of igniting the joy of learning English in children by applying creative teaching methods. The content emphasizes communication skills on familiar topics (such as greetings, objects, and everyday activities) to provide students with vital English knowledge for daily conversations.

After weeks of teaching, a school-wide English fair was held with the support of nearly 70 school teachers and Vinuni students. The fair provides opportunities for students to apply what they have learned within the project by participating in many interesting English games and challenges. According to Ms. Nguyet, Vice Principal of Hung Dao Primary School, this is a meaningful and practical project for the locality and small provincial public primary schools, where English communication skills still receive little attention.
